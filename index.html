About Us

Christian Music Hub is a world-class central repository of pure Christian music worldwide. Made by Christian artists for every race, for every nation, for every color, for everyone. All music and other corresponding resources are vetted to be of good morals, promotes godliness and are child-friendly.

With Christian Music Hub, it’s easy to find the right music for every activity – for your worship services, events, acoustic moments and many more.

On your phone, your computer, your tablet and more. It contains thousands of tracks; old and new. Whether you’re driving, riding, working out, partying or relaxing, the right music is always at your fingertips. Choose what you want to listen to, or let Christian Music Hub surprise you.

You can also be able to just sit back after creating your own playlist to enjoy or be shared with your friends.

Also available are lyrics, minus 1, song stories and artists’ biographies.

Subscribe now and build your playlist that promotes God through music.

Our Purpose

In line with our parent organization’s purpose, Christian Music Hub will be a network of Christian artists and musicians which aims to unite believers from all denominations to influence and lead the world to worship our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.

To provide Christian artists worldwide a platform to promote their music to their actual market. And for consumers, to be assured of a platform that is world-class, affordable, free from compromises and irrelevant contents.
